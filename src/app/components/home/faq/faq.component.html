Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is MovieTheater?

    MovieTheater is the place where the art of cinema merges with modern comfort. With four theaters equipped with the latest audio and video technologies, we invite you to immerse yourself in a world of engaging stories. Our programming embraces diversity, offering a wide range of movies, TV series, anime, and documentaries. What makes Slythefilms truly unique? No advertising interruptions! Watch as much as you want, whenever you want, all at an affordable monthly price. Discover something new, with new movies and shows added every week!

  • How much does a ticket cost?

    We have two types of tickets. A standard ticket for adults priced at €10. A reduced ticket for children up to 12 years old priced at €5.

  • When is the schedule updated?

    The schedule is updated between Wednesday evening and Thursday.

  • How can I write a review?

    From the movie details section, click on the 'Add a review' button. You can view reviews from all users on their profile page.

  • How can I reserve a seat in the theater?

    To reserve a seat, simply follow these steps:
    - Login to your MovieTheater account.
    - Choose the movie and showtime you want to attend.
    - Select the desired seat(s) on the seating chart.
    - Complete the reservation process by confirming your selection.
    Enjoy your movie experience with the assurance of a reserved seat!
